Welcome to The Vip Park(Chennai)

Welcome to the business class hotel with state of the art facilities and state of the heart Hospitality. Conveniently located on Chennai and within walking distance from the Chennai central railway station.

Deluxe Single rooms

The Magnificent Kingsize Single and Twin sharing Double rooms are designed with a perfect ambience to unwind the day and relax with comfort. Our Rooms include a magnificent master bedroom and two marble with a luxurious bath including Jacuzzi and rain shower. 64 Deluxe Singles & 73 Deluxe Double Rooms of 175 Sq Ft to 225 Sq Ft each are located at the 1st / 2nd / 3rd / 4th and 5th floors of THE VIP PARK.

Family Deluxe Rooms

Family Deluxe rooms are specially designed for a perfect family stay with 2 King–Size Beds and located near the lift of each floor. The room categories enjoy magnificent views of either the long 100ft road or city view with big buildings, towers and parks. The 5 Family Deluxe rooms of 250 Sq Ft each are located at the 1st / 2nd / 3rd / 4th and 5th floors of THE VIP PARK.

Presidential Suites

Your Private Suite within the Hotel, the Presidential Suites represent luxury at its finest comprising a spacious living room with elegant dining and a Magnificent Bedroom. The living room is spacious and adorned with soft hues of gold and Brown clothed furniture and fine lighted with chandeliers. It will lead you into a plush Bedroom decorated with white and brown silks. The 2 Presidential Suites of 650 Sq.ft each are located at the 1st and 2nd floors of THE VIP PARK.
